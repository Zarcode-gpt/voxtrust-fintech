The Problem

Africa's Trade is Broken by
a Lack of Trust

Across Nigeria and Africa, informal trade is riddled with inefficiencies and fraud that cost businesses billions every year.

⚠️

Frequent Fraud & Disputes

Payment scams and trade disputes are rampant, leaving traders with no structured resolution mechanism.

🚫

No Identity Verification

Traders operate anonymously — there's no way to verify who you're dealing with before a transaction.

📍

Zero Shipment Visibility

Goods move across hundreds of kilometres with no real-time tracking, creating uncertainty and loss.

📵

Poor Coordination

Traders, transporters, and buyers rely on unstructured communication channels that break down easily.

🏦

No Access to Financial Tools

Most informal traders lack access to structured financial services that can help them grow and manage risk.
